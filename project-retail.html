Operations Software for Retail

A live internal operations platform built for a retail business — streamlining inventory management, order tracking, and team workflows to measurably improve day-to-day efficiency.

Project Type
Live Product
Industry
Retail / Operations
Tools Used
Jira, Confluence

The Problem

The retail business was managing inventory, orders, and staff coordination through a mix of WhatsApp messages, spreadsheets, and verbal communication. This caused missed orders, stock discrepancies, and a lack of visibility across the team.

My Role

I led the product side of building an internal operations tool. My responsibilities included:

  • Conducting discovery sessions with staff to understand daily workflows
  • Defining requirements and writing clear user stories
  • Creating the product roadmap and prioritising features by impact
  • Working with the development team to deliver and iterate

What Was Built

  • Centralised inventory tracking with low-stock alerts
  • Order management dashboard visible to all team members
  • Role-based access so staff only see what's relevant to them
  • Activity log so managers can track all actions taken

The Outcome

The platform is live and actively used by the business. It reduced miscommunication significantly and gave management real-time visibility into operations for the first time — replacing the chaos of informal tools with a single source of truth.
